Welcome to the Cuore Rossonero World!


The one card to truly live your passion for AC Milan.
Cuore Rossonero is the best way to experience everything being a Rossoneri fan has to offer. It makes your stadium experience a breeze: with exclusive sales points* available to cardholders following the team on the road or attending games with law enforcement restrictions won’t be an issue. Cuore Rossonero can be activated with your personal season pass or with single match tickets. But wait, there’s more! Through the Change User function, a season ticket holder can pass on match tickets for a specific game to your card. To access the exclusive ticketing services and promotions available to cardholders, make triple sure all your personal information is up-to-date and you’ve read, understood and accepted the terms and conditions. Your MyMilan account is the hub through which the club can keep in touch with you, allowing us to provide you with tailored offers and keeping you informed on everything about AC Milan, please ensure you keep your personal details up-to-date.
